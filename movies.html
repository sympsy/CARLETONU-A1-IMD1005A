Instead of Movies: Reasons why YOU should get a Cat

Reason Explanation
Independence Cats are content to entertain themselves, requiring less constant attention.
Low Maintenance No need for daily walks, grooming is minimal, and litter box training is generally easier.
Quiet Companions Meows are generally softer and less frequent than barking.
Cleanliness Cats are meticulous groomers, requiring fewer baths.
Adaptability Cats thrive in smaller spaces and are content with indoor lifestyles.
